## What is LabForger?

LabForger is my personal technical blog about networking, homelab projects, infrastructure, security, and the occasional piece of hardware that probably should have been left alone.

Most posts are project write-ups: what I tried, what broke, what worked, and what I learned along the way.

## Topics I Write About

* Networking
* Homelabs
* Infrastructure
* Hardware
* Security
* Troubleshooting
* Practical IT learning
